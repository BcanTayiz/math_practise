# Corrolaries of Axioms

**Def**: Group G is a set * operation 

```math
(1) \,\,\forall g1,g2,g3  \to (g1,g2)g3= g1(g2,g3)  \\[10px]

(2)\,\,\exists e \in G : \forall g \in G \\[10px]
e \cdot g = g \cdot e = g \\[10px]

(3)\,\, \forall g \in G\,\, \exists g^{'} \in G\\[10px]
gg^{'} = g{'}g = e


```

## Some Corralaries

1) Unit is unique

**PF** e1 and e2 are units then,

```math

e_{1} = e_{1} e_{2} = e_{2}

```

2) Inverse is unique

```math

(g^{'}g)g^{''} = eg^{''} \\[10px]
g^{'}(gg^{''}) \to g^{'}e \to g' = g''

```

3) 

```math
\forall\,\, g_{1}, g_{2} \in G \\[10px]
(g_{1},g_{2})^{-1} = (g_{1}^{-1},g_{2}^{-1})

```

4) Cancellation Property

```math
\forall\,\, g_{1}, g_{2} \in G \\[10px]
if \,\, g_{1}h = g_{2}h \implies g_{1} = g_{2}

```

5) Equations have unique solutions
```math
x = g_{1}g_{2} \implies xg_{1} = g_{2}\,\, and \,\, g_{1}x = g_{2} \\[10px]
\text{have unique solutions}
```
